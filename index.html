Seanema Film Festival screening 'The Hunting Ground' in Tirana

Imagine a “hunting ground”. Perfect storm conditions for predators to prey with impunity. A pile of victims and among them - two survivors taking matters into their own hands, ingeniously employing a legal strategy to fight back. Young women chose to no longer stay silent. This is happening in the United States of America. How is Albania coming to grips with sexual assault? “In order for the light to shine so brightly, the darkness must be present.” Join us for the screening of THE HUNTING GROUND at Reja Pavillion!
